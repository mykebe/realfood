Milch

Best:Saubere Rohvollmilch von zertifizierten gesunden Weidetieren, Kuhmilch vorzugsweise von altmodischen Rassen, die fettreiche Milch produzieren, wie Jerseys und Guernseys.

Good:Full-fat, pasteurized, non-homogenized milk from grass-fed animals, without added vitamins. This milk is best if made into cultured dairy products such as yogurt and kefir.

Avoid:Lowfat and skim milk; ultra-paseurized milk; homogenized milk; flavored milk like chocolate milk; milk from animals that arenot graass-fed: milk with added vitamins; processed imitation milk made from soy, rice, almonds, oats, hemp, etc.
